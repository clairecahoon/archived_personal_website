Digital Pedagogy · Digital Humanities · Programming Education · Academic Libraries · Accessible Resources

I'm a first-year graduate student pursuing a Masters of Science in Information Science from the School of Information and Library Science at UNC Chapel Hill, expected to graduate in 2020.

I'm interested in digital pedagogy, both to teach classes with technology components and also to facilitate the use of digital tools in other types of learning and instruction. I'm also interested in digital humanities and non-traditional forms of research, especially how libraries can provide guidance to help people of all kinds understand the impact of research. In my coursework, I focus on programming tools with pedagogy and digital scholarship in mind, and I have an interest in how code and other technical skills are taught.
